Pizza's Recipe

It's easy to make this thin crust pizza dough recipe with a few ingredients you may likely already have on hand. Here's what you'll need:

Ingredients

  • Yeast: This thin crust pizza dough starts with one teaspoon of active dry yeast. /li>
  • Sugar and water Activate the yeast in white sugar and lukewarm water./li>
  • Flour: This crispy thin crust calls for all-purpose flour instead of bread flour.
  • Salt: Salt tightens the gluten structure, which strengthens the dough and makes it more elastic.
